Who I Am

Hi, my name is Colin Bruin and I am a web developer from Zionsville, IN. I have been developing for roughly about a year and a half now and couldn't consider doing anything else. I graduated from Zionsville High School in 2012 and attended Indiana University where I majored in Economics. Although I enjoyed economics and the prospects ahead, I knew something was missing. I took my first computer course at IU for an easy A, and immediately fell in love with coding. I graduated from IU and then attended Purdue University where I recieved a certificate in Web Development. This website aims to provide a more in-depth illustration of my work and my life. It will be continually updated and upgraded so do not be suprised to find small or even large changes. If you need help with a website, or would just like to learn about web development, do not hesitate to contact me. For any questions or comments, also do not hesitate to contact me.
